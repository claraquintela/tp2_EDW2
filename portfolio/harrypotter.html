Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - part 2

This shot was developed using Maya and standard maya particles. I was responsible for the wand magic fx. The fx were done using particles emit from geometry on the wand and then using motion blur and prman render enging I rendered and processed the particles through Nuke to give it a more glowy and magical feel. As a result the shot came out menacing and supernatural.
